Add an optional limited guesses mode that adds tension and challenge.

When enabled, players have a finite number of guesses based on difficulty:
- Easy (1-50): 8 guesses
- Medium (1-100): 10 guesses
- Hard (1-500): 13 guesses
- Insane (1-1000): 15 guesses

Togglable between relaxed (unlimited) and challenge (limited) mode.
